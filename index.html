About Me

Intro

Hello! I'm Rahul Chauhan, a Bengaluru native with a deep passion for web development and technology. My journey in tech began at Laxmi Institute of Technology Sarigam, where I earned my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering.

Currently, I'm honing my skills in the MERN stack and responsive web design at AccioJob bootcamp. My professional path includes digital marketing and web development at Pixelation, and database design at Softyoug. Outside of work, I'm a stock market enthusiast and an aspiring tech entrepreneur.
